# Cryptodyne Instructions (SOPs)

## SOP-01: Trade Execution
1. Verify liquidity is sufficient for position size.
2. Check spread is within 0.1% tolerance.
3. Confirm no major news events in the next 15 minutes.

## SOP-02: Compliance Check
1. Query the asset against the permissible list.
2. Verify funding rate is neutral or positive (if long).
3. Log the compliance check ID.
