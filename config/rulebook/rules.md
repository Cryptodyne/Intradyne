# Cryptodyne Rulebook (v1.0)

## Fundamental Rules
1. **Capital Preservation**: Never risk more than 2% of total equity on a single trade.
2. **Shariah Compliance**: All trades must pass the AAOIFI screening process.
3. **Trend Alignment**: Do not trade against the H4 trend unless in a specific mean-reversion regime.
4. **Stop Loss**: Every trade must have a hard stop loss at entry.
5. **No FOMO**: If the setup is not perfect, do not trade.

## Risk Management
10. **Drawdown Limit**: If daily drawdown exceeds 5%, halt trading for 24 hours.
11. **Leverage Cap**: Maximum leverage for perps is 1x (effectively spot) unless specific conditions met (max 3x).
